Lecture Information

Your students are listed here. The list is retreived from your institution's network, to verify that only legitimate students are signing into your class.

Once you begin marking, your device will act as the endpoint for your students to connect to.

To see how this works, an animation will play upon clicking Start Marking.

Step 2 of 3

Await Sign-ins

Once you've opened up for marking, your students will be able to connect to your device and mark themselves present.

If there are any troubles with a phone's connection, and you are certain of their presence, you can mark a student manually by clicking their item in the list.
